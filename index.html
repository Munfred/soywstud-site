What is Soylent?

A rich, creamy, and strangly satisfying beverage with all of the nutrients of a meal.

Soylent is a brand of meal replacement products that are advertised for consumption as "staple meals". Soylent uses science and technology to solve the challenges plaguing the current food system and work toward its mission of providing access to quality nutrition to people across the globe. Jammed packed with vitamins, minerals, and nutrients, Soylent provides the best late night meal, early morning treat and midday hunger-curer.
